Welcome !!

Welcome to Hygienic Tanks, your trusted partner in maintaining clean and safe water storage for healthier living. Established in 2013, our mission is to ensure the cleanliness of drinking water tanks and sumps reservoirs through scientific cleaning methods and the use of sophisticated equipment.Water is an essential part of our daily routine, serving various purposes like bathing, brushing, washing, and cooking. It is crucial to store water in a hygienic place where it remains uncontaminated and free from bacteria. Surprisingly, around 60% of diseases that affect humans are waterborne. However, the proper maintenance and regular cleaning of water storage tanks and sumps can effectively control these issues.
