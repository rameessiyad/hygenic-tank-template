About Us

Hygienic Tank is steadfast in its commitment to providing top-notch cleaning services, guaranteeing superior quality and customer satisfaction.

Welcome to Hygienic Tanks, your trusted partner in maintaining clean and safe water storage for healthier living. Established in 2013, our mission is to ensure the cleanliness of drinking water tanks and sumps reservoirs through scientific cleaning methods and the use of sophisticated equipment Water is an essential part of our daily routine, serving various purposes like bathing, brushing, washing, and cooking.

It is crucial to store water in a hygienic place where it remains uncontaminated and free from bacteria. Surprisingly, around 60% of diseases that affect humans are waterborne. However, the proper maintenance and regular cleaning of water storage tanks and sumps can effectively control these issues.

The cleaning of water tanks can be accomplished through both manual and scientific processes, ensuring a hygienic and safe water supply.


Manual Cleaning Process

The manual cleaning process involves three stages:

  1. Cleaning the tank thoroughly.
  2. Disinfecting the tank with chlorine for 12 to 24 hours.
  3. Checking the residual chlorine concentration, ensuring it remains at 0.5mg/l.

While this method is effective, it does have some drawbacks, including its time-consuming nature and water wastage in the cleaning process.

Scientific Cleaning process

Our preferred method, the scientific cleaning process, involves a comprehensive approach to ensure thorough cleanliness and minimal water wastage. The steps include:

  1. High-pressure motor jet cleaning of the tank caps and surroundings.
  2. Dewatering the tank and removing sludge with a sludge pump.
  3. Eliminating algae and other substances from the tank's ceiling and walls.
  4. Using a vacuum sucker machine to remove tiny particles from the ceiling.
  5. Sterilizing the tank with a safe anti-bacterial agent to disinfect all surfaces.
  6. Treating the tank with ozone or ultraviolet rays for complete removal of microbial bacteria, if any.
  7. Cleaning the tank thoroughly.

This scientific cleaning process not only saves time but also reduces water wastage significantly.

In the past, water tanks and sumps were often cleaned by untrained and unprofessional daily laborers using unhygienic broom sticks and scrubbers. Unfortunately, this resulted in impurities and bacteria lingering in the tank even after cleaning. However, with Hygienic Tanks, you can rest assured that your water storage will be professionally and meticulously cleaned, ensuring a safe and clean water supply.
